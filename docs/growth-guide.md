What makes it work: you offer something (feedback), not just a link.

## Part 3 — Content Strategy (What to Post)

### Content pillars (rotate through these)
1. **Real portfolio showcases** — "Meet [Name], a Social Media Manager from Lagos. She built her portfolio in 4 minutes. See it here: [link]" + screenshot
2. **Before/after** — "This is what her profile looked like before. This is what recruiters see now."
3. **Tips for job hunters** — "3 things recruiters check before they even read your CV"
4. **Behind the scenes** — "We just helped our 10th user go live. Here's what they said."
5. **Questions that start conversations** — "Do you have a portfolio link? Drop it below — I'll give you feedback."

### Posting schedule (start here, adjust as you learn)
| Day | Content type |
|-----|-------------|
| Monday | Portfolio showcase (real user or demo) |
| Wednesday | Job hunting tip or stat |
| Friday | Question post — invite engagement |

Don't post every day at the start. 3x per week, consistent, is better than 7x for 2 weeks then nothing.

### Caption formula that works
```
[Relatable hook — 1 line]
[The problem — 1–2 lines]
[What liveportfolio.site does — 1 line]
[Call to action — soft, not pushy]
[Link in comments or in caption]
```

Example:
> Recruiters Google your name before they call you.
> If nothing comes up, they move to the next candidate.
> liveportfolio.site puts your best work front and center — in 5 minutes.
> Build yours free today → liveportfolio.site
> Drop your current portfolio below if you have one. I'll give feedback 👇

### What NOT to post
- Don't post "Check out my product!" with no context
- Don't drop links in comments of other people's posts without being invited
- Don't post the same caption twice in the same group
- Don't add people to WhatsApp groups without permission

## Part 4 — Group Posting Strategy (the growth engine)

This is where the real early users come from.

### The process (do this 2–3x per week)
1. Find a relevant group (tech, career, job hunting, alumni)
2. Join and wait 3–5 days before posting
3. Engage first — like and comment on 2–3 posts genuinely
4. Post using the message formula above (adapt it to the group)
5. Monitor the post for comments — reply to every single one within 2 hours

### How to avoid being flagged
- Never post the same text in multiple groups on the same day
- Always rewrite the caption slightly for each group
- Don't post links in groups that forbid them — post the message, then say "DM me for the link"
- Space out group posts: max 3 groups per day, at least 2 hours apart
- Engage with other people's posts in the group before and after posting yours

### When someone comments asking for more info
Reply warmly. Example:
> "Hey [Name]! Yes absolutely — it's completely free to preview. You just fill in your info, AI generates the copy, and you can see your portfolio live before paying anything. The link is liveportfolio.site — try it and let me know how it goes!"

## Part 5 — Using User-Generated Content

Every satisfied user is marketing material. When someone shares their portfolio link:

1. **Ask for a screenshot and a one-liner** — "Can I share your experience? Just 1 sentence about what you liked."
2. **Post it with their permission** — "Meet [First name], a [Role] from [City]. She built her portfolio in [X] minutes. She said: '[quote]'. See it: [link]"
3. **Tag them if they're on Facebook** — their network sees it. Free reach.

Template message to send users:
> "Hi [Name]! Your portfolio looks great 🎉 Would you be okay if I shared it on our Facebook page as an example? Just drop a one-liner about your experience and I'll post it. Totally optional but it helps others see what's possible!"

- Be 600–900 words (not long, just useful)
- End with a CTA: "Build your own portfolio free at liveportfolio.site"
- Be shared to all Facebook groups and WhatsApp

First 4 posts to write:
